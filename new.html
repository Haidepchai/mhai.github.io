Ho Chi Minh

Ho Chi Minh City, often referred to as Saigon, is the largest city in Vietnam. It's a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and delicious cuisine. Some key highlights include

  • Ben Thanh Market
  • War Remnants Museum
  • Independence Palace

Ha Noi

Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful architecture. Hanoi offers a unique blend of traditional and modern elements, making it a fascinating destination for both locals and visitors. Some key highlights include:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Ol Quater
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum
  • Temple of Literature
